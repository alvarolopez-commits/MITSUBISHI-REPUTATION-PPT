05 Recommendations

High-priority actions

01
High priority / After-sales

Standardise booking and waiting-time handling

Create a common after-sales journey standard covering appointment confirmation, service start-time, delay updates and vehicle handover.

02
High priority / After-sales

Build a service recovery protocol

Define clear ownership, response timing and closure rules for complaints, especially when maintenance or waiting-time issues escalate.

03
High priority / Sales

Close the Sales experience gap

Audit the sales journey around expectation setting, pricing transparency, staff professionalism and delivery follow-up.

05 Recommendations

Medium-priority actions

04
Medium priority / After-sales

Export after-sales routines from the strongest dealers

Use high-performing Mitsubishi dealers to identify repeatable after-sales behaviours: reception, advisor communication, service updates and final handover.

05
High or Medium priority / Both

Codify Staff Friendliness and Professionalism

Turn the strongest positive driver into a network playbook: greeting, explanation, empathy, proactive updates and closing interaction.

06
Medium priority / Both

Create a review-generation learning loop

Standardise when and how dealers invite customers to leave feedback after positive service moments, while using review content to identify behaviours worth replicating.

Longer horizon: validate before scaling network-wide

07
Longer horizon / After-sales

Turn Service and Maintenance into a quality programme
